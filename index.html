The Cult of Innovation

Fermi Center for Applied Sciences is an independent research entity, established is association of Minus Zero, to nurture frugal and indigenous innovation & research in India across the domains of Applied Engineering, Life Sciences and Engineering.

Students are selected for the fellowship program through Call-for-Proposals, and the  ones with best & novel ideas are invited to remotely work at the center to develop and document their project and simultaneously qualify towards work experience for their profiles. 

Hands-on experience

The students focus on building a robust prototype, and learn by doing instead of just theoretical study. 

Project Incubation

The students get mentor support and resources to complete their research or engineering projects within timelines.

Industrial Validation

The projects are consistently aligned with the market & industrial demand to make sure students add value to the community.

Make in India

The initiative aims to nurture indigenous  innovation-focused mindset to help put India on the global map, and eventually helping it become a technology leader. 

Liberty of Exploration

The students are free to choose any idea they wish to work upon and submit proposal accordingly. Innovation can't be bound by constraint of choices.

Talent Outreach

The projects undertaken are properly documented, and students earn proper experience credentials to gain a competitive edge in the reality.
